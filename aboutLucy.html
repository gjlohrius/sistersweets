ABOUT LUCY

"I started baking with my grandmother teaching me. She had a lot of old recipes that were written down; some that she made and some that were passed down in her family. I really loved how much we got to spend time together when baking so I always wanted to bake with her. Eventually, I got old enough and wanted to try it more myself, and I don't get to bake as much with my grandmother anymore. But I have grown to love baking on my own because I get to make all the decisions and I have learned to do some recipes from the heart and not measure anything out.

I also grew to love baking so much because of my brother who loves my desserts and because they are so delicious so anyone can enjoy them. I also bake to take a little break and just focus on making something and not like school or anything else.

I eventually worked towards decorating what I baked, like cupcakes and sugar cookies, and kept getting better at it with practice. I then started the business for friends and families to bake whatever they wanted with full customization. I love doing this because I get to be very creative and feel proud of my creations." `
